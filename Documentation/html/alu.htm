ARITHMETIC AND LOGIC UNIT (ALU)

 

   This is the area of the microprocessor where various computing functions are performed on data.

   The ALU performs arithmetic operations such as addition and subtraction and logical operations such as AND, OR and exclusive OR.

   It includes the accumulator, the temporary registers, the arithmetic and logic circuits and the five FLAGS.

   The temporary registers are used to hold data during an arithmetic or logic operation.

   The result is stored in the accumulator and the flags are set or reset according to the result of the operation.
